About NFJPIA?

NFJPIA (National Federation of Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants) is the official national organization of students taking up accountancy and other accounting-related curriculum. It is the only national student organization fully recognized and accredited by the Philippine Institute of Certified Public Accountants. It continues to rank as the country’s largest and one of the Philippine’s premiere student organization.

HUGYAW

Every federation year has its peculiar identity. This batch seeks to emphasize the unity in diversity from all around the Philippines pursuing goals as one. HUGYAW, a Cebuano word, meaning,“roar”, is chosen to give most significance to the whole body of NFJPIA pursuing the same avenues, roaring as one.
