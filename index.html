About Us

________________________


The National Federation of Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants aspires to be an integral part of nation building by reinventing the Filipino youth. This institution provides an avenue for academic excellence and social growth for the accountancy students with the aim to be a human resource development center of future leaders and professionals - appreciating and refining raw talent and potential - thus supplying quality workforce worthy of global recognition.
