NFJPIA LOGO

The colors and definition of the Federation Seal shall be as follows:

Gold for the wheel and the map, which signifies the essentiality of each local chapter of the federation;

Royal blue for the background color which signifies integrity, dignity, and professionalism;

White for the ledger which signifies fairness and accuracy; and

Green for the laurel leaves, which signifies excellence.

The figures of the Federation Seal and their definitions are as follows:

a. The six (6) spokes represent the six (6) founding schools of NFJPIA, namely: De La Salle University (Manila), Philippine Christian University (Manila), Philippine School of Business Administration (Manila), St. Scholastica’s College (Manila), College of the Holy Spirit (Manila), University of the Philippines (Diliman, Quezon City);

b. The wheel represents the Federation’s continuing efforts to develop JPIANs into well- balanced individuals;

c. The laurel leaves (33 on the side) shall symbolize intelligence, growing knowledge, and academic excellence; and

d. The ledger and the map shall represent the accountancy students who are members of the federation.

Siuala LOGO

Every federation year has its peculiar identity. This batch seeks to uphold the voice of every JPIAn and herald the same into avenues that will truly be beneficial to our collective well-roundedness. SIUALA, a Kapampangan word, meaning, "voice", truly fits this noble aspiration. As in our theme, "Isang Tinig, Isang Pintig: 34 Years of Resonating the JPIAn Commitment to Holistic Excellence".

SIX-POINT AGENDA:

1. ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE: Adapting best practices towards the advancement of educational well-roundedness.

2. SKILLS & TALENT ENRICHMENT: Creating an avenue to showcase and harness the holistic potentials of JPIAns.

3. FISCAL SUSTAINABILITY & TRANSPARENCY: Creating measures to ensure the overall financial health of the federation and being able to faithfully disclose such to its constituency.

4. SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY: Acknowledging the role of the organization as co-builder in   making the greater community a better place.

5. LEADERSHIP & GRASSROOT EMPOWERMENT: Strengthening the core of the federation and forging ties that transcend professional norms

6. PROFESSIONAL LINKAGES Valuing and nurturing mutual relationships with partners who play an integral role in the furtherance of the organization's pursuits
